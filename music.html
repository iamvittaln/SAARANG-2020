SAARANG MUSIC COMPETITION

QUARANTUNES

Competitions:

  1. SYNCHROTUNE

    2. Be prepared to be transported to every nook and corner of the majestic country, India.
    Showcase your exhilarating and stage-rocking music at the eastern band event. Explore the colourful harmonic caves
    of the nation and take your audience on a trip, quite literally. Pack your bags, grab your instruments and get ready for the musical ‘Adventure of a Lifetime’.

    RULES & OTHER INFORMATION:

    • A Band can have a maximum of 12 members on stage.
    • Every band is allowed 18 minutes of stage time incuding setup.
    • A standard 5 piece kit, a keyboard stand and amps will be provided.
    • The use of arpeggiators and pre-recorded beats are not allowed.
    • Own compositions will be awarded extra points.
    • Bands will be judged based on their tightness, clarity and dynamics.
    • No form of vulgarity will be tolerated on stage.

  2. CARNATIC - SOLO Vocal

    3. Devotion can be enjoyed, felt and rejoiced through music. We welcome you to participate in our event
    to celebrate the enchanting renditions in this rich cultural heritage. Let’s come together in the spirit of music!

    RULES & OTHER INFORMATION:

    • Participants have to perform a Carnatic or Hindustani piece only.
    • Each participant will be given 5 minutes on stage.
    • Accompaniments are not allowed.
    • Ragam, Neraval and Kalpanaswaram will fetch extra points.
    • A shruti box will be provided during the event.
    • The judge’s decision will be final.

  3. WESTERN - SOLO Vocal

    4. Do you think that you are the next Adele, Chris Martin, Rihanna or John Mayer?
    Do you want to break away from the crowd and go solo?
    Look no further! This is your ticket to take us across the globe and enthral us with the magic of your voice.

    RULES & OTHER INFORMATION:

    •Participants will have to perform songs in English only.
    •Each participant will be given 5 minutes on stage.
    •Either a karaoke track or one accompanying instrument is allowed.
    •The judge’s decision will be final.

  4. INSTRUMENTAL - EASTERN

    5. Travel across the ethnic dimensions of Indian music and experience the thrill of Raagas and different Taalas.
    Show the world that you are indeed the true master of your instrument. We invite all the aspiring musicians out there
    to embark on a journey and to rediscover eastern instrumental music.

    RULES & OTHER INFORMATION:

    • Participants will have to perform a Carnatic or Hindustani piece only.
    • Each participant is given a time limit of 5 minutes onstage.
    • The judge's decision will be final.
    • Film songs played as instrumental are strictly not allowed.
    • A keyboard stand and guitar amps will be provided.

  5. INSTRUMENTAL - WESTERN

    6. Do you think that you are the next Cory Henry, Michael League or Mark Lettieri?
    Do your hands itch to play the best solo that this world has ever heard?
    Itch no further. Plug in your instrument and blow our minds with amazing riffs and delicate runs at SAARANG's western instrumental event.

    RULES & OTHER INFORMATION:

    • Participants will have to perform a Western piece only.
    • Each participant is given a time limit of 5 minutes onstage.
    • The judge's decision will be final.
    • Film songs played as instrumental are strictly not allowed.
    • A keyboard stand and guitar amps will be provided.
